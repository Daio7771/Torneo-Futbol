Daniel Blanxart Institute - Soccer Tournament The Daniel Blanxart Institute is organizing a football tournament on May 29, 2026.

Teams will consist of a minimum of 5 and a maximum of 9 players, 5 of whom will be on the field at any one time. Mixed teams and teams composed of students from different classes are welcome.

To register, please send your team name and a list of players to Professor Pau Martínez.

We encourage everyone to participate!
